Introducing the Revuelto

Unleashed just before Lamborghini’s 60th anniversary, the Revuelto is a revolutionary HPEV hybrid supercar. With a combined output of 1015 hp from a V12 engine and three electric motors, it’s the future of speed and sustainability.

It features a brand-new dual-clutch gearbox, groundbreaking aerodynamics, and the soul of a true racing machine.
